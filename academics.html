Our Curriculum

AI-First Education

Cambridge curriculum meets cutting-edge technology. Every subject is infused with hands-on AI and robotics, preparing children to become builders and innovators.

Flagship Program

Pakistan's Most Advanced Early Robotics Program

From LEGO to Arduino to AI — every child becomes a builder, not just a learner.

Grades 1-3: LEGO Foundations

Young builders start with LEGO Mindstorms and WeDo kits. Problem-solving through play. Engineering concepts without the jargon.

Grades 4-6: Arduino & Sensors

Students code Arduino microcontrollers. Build robots that sense light, temperature, and movement. Real electronics, real impact.

Grades 7-8: 3D Design & AI

CAD design, 3D printing, and introduction to machine learning. Students design, iterate, and create. Competition-ready.

Every Child Gets Hands-On Time

  • Weekly robotics sessions for all grades
  • Competition teams for all interest levels
  • LEGO, Arduino, 3D printers — always available
  • Expert robotics faculty trained in STEM pedagogy
  • Annual robotics showcases where kids demo their creations
  • STEM scholarship opportunities for excellence
