Stop 01 The Arrival

Your First Impression

You arrive on Ali Road, Township, and the building catches your eye immediately. It's well-maintained and alive — there's an energy here that's hard to describe. You can sense it before you've even stepped through the gate.

The front of London School — a building that reflects the care put into everything inside it
What you'll notice first

A school that cares shows it from the outside

Most parents tell us the same thing: "We knew before we even walked in." The grounds are kept, the signage is clear, and there's always a staff member near the gate to greet you. It's the small things that tell you a school takes pride in itself.

London School sits in a residential neighbourhood — surrounded by families, not traffic. Mornings here have a rhythm. Children arriving on time, teachers ready, the day already in motion.

Even after hours, the school feels warm and active

Stop 03 The Classrooms

Where Learning Happens

Open, bright, and built for thinking. Each classroom has a digital whiteboard, natural light from wide windows, and enough space that children can breathe and move. Your child would sit here, every day, and grow.

Classrooms designed for interaction — not just rows of chairs, but space for discussion, collaboration, and discovery
Small classes, real attention

Every child is known here

Class sizes are capped so teachers can give each student what they actually need — not what works for the average. Your child's teacher will know their name, their learning style, and their favourite subject before the first month is out.

The principal's office has an open-door policy. Parents can book a check-in anytime. Issues don't fester here — they're addressed with the same warmth you see everywhere else in the school.

Stop 07 Spaces & Facilities

Every Room Has a Purpose

Purpose-built for learning, playing, and growing — here's a closer look at the spaces that make London School what it is.

Main Building

Smart Classrooms

Bright, airy classrooms equipped with digital whiteboards, interactive learning tools, and a layout designed for collaboration — not just rows of desks.

Library

The Knowledge Hub

A fully stocked library with reading corners, group study tables, and a stunning mural that captures our philosophy perfectly.

Robotics Lab

Build. Break. Learn.

LEGO Mindstorms, Arduino boards, 3D printers, and a tool wall. Students from Grade 1 upwards get hands-on time here every week.

Computer Lab

AI & Coding Workstations

Dedicated computers for every student, running Scratch, Python, and AI learning environments. Where the next generation of builders starts.

Early Years

Indoor Play Area

A vibrant indoor play space for nursery and KG children — slides, ball pits, and colourful murals make every day feel like a celebration.

Assembly Hall

Events & Gatherings

A spacious hall where the whole school comes together — morning assemblies, student speeches, cultural days, and prize-giving ceremonies.

Music Room

Rhythm & Expression

A dedicated music room where children discover their creative voice — singing, movement, instruments, and performance from an early age.

Sports & Play

25+ Sports Activities

From cricket and football to martial arts and athletics — our outdoor spaces give children the room to run, compete, and build confidence.
