The Process

How to Enrol

Four simple steps. Most families are enrolled within a week.

1

Book a Campus Tour

Come see the school in person. Walk through the classrooms, meet teachers, and let your child explore. WhatsApp us or call to schedule — it's free and takes about 45 minutes.

2

Complete the Application Form

A short form to understand your child's age, current grade level, and any special requirements. Available at the school office or on request via WhatsApp.

3

Informal Assessment

A friendly, low-pressure session where we get to know your child — not a test, just a conversation. Helps us place them in the right class and set them up for success from day one.

4

Confirm Enrolment

Submit the required documents (birth certificate, previous school reports) and the registration fee. Your child's place is confirmed and they can start on the agreed date.
