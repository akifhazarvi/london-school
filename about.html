About Us

Built on the Legacy of Prof. Waris Mir

A warm, nurturing Cambridge school in Lahore where children explore AI, build robots, play sports, and discover what they love.

Welcome

Message from the Principal

Welcome to London School, Pakistan's premier AI & robotics school. We believe that in a world transformed by technology, every child deserves to be a builder, not just a consumer. AI and robotics aren't add-ons to our curriculum — they're foundational. Every subject, from language to science, is infused with hands-on, real-world problem-solving.

Here, your child becomes confident in technology while remaining deeply human. We develop critical thinkers, compassionate leaders, and curious builders. Small class sizes, teachers who truly know each student, and learning environments where mistakes fuel discovery — that's how we nurture the next generation of innovators.

We'd love to meet you and your child. Come experience the London School difference firsthand.

— Mehr un Nisa Masood
Principal, London School System

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

These principles, rooted in Prof. Waris Mir's philosophy, guide every decision we make.

Curiosity First

We teach children to ask great questions — to wonder, experiment, and discover. In a world of answers, we celebrate the courage to ask.

Every Child Matters

Small class sizes. Teachers who know every child's name, strengths, and struggles. We see each student as a whole person, not just a test score.

Ready for the World

Cambridge curriculum, three foreign languages, AI and robotics, 25+ sports. We prepare children for a future we can't yet predict.

Our Mission

To develop creative, ethical, digitally intelligent learners through an AI-first education model. We nurture young minds who don't just adapt to technology — they shape it. Every child leaves London School with the confidence to think critically, code creatively, and lead with integrity.

Our Vision

To be Pakistan's leading institution that empowers globally aware, morally grounded, technologically confident learners. A place where children become builders of tomorrow — where curiosity is celebrated, mistakes fuel growth, and education is both rigorous and joyful.

Education is not about filling empty vessels. It's about lighting fires that burn for a lifetime. Every child carries within them the spark of genius. Our job is to fan that flame.
— Prof. Waris Mir
