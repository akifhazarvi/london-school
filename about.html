Our Heritage

Built on a Legacy of Vision

London School is rooted in the life and philosophy of Prof. Waris Mir — an educator who believed that every child, regardless of background, deserves access to world-class education and the chance to discover their potential.

The Founder

Prof. Waris Mir (1938–1987)

A visionary ahead of his time. Prof. Waris Mir dedicated his life to reimagining education in South Asia. In an era when quality schooling was the privilege of the few, he built institutions rooted in the belief that curiosity, creativity, and compassion were every child's birthright.

His philosophy was simple but radical: Learning should feel like discovery, not memorisation. Classrooms should be warm spaces where every child felt known. Education should prepare children for a world that hadn't been invented yet — not just the world as it was.

Though Prof. Mir passed away in 1987, his legacy lives on in every classroom, every student, and every decision we make at London School. His vision guides us still.

Our Journey

A Timeline of Growth

1987

Prof. Waris Mir's Legacy Begins

Though Prof. Mir passed away this year, his educational philosophy and ideals became the foundation upon which London School would be built by those inspired by his vision.

2010

London School is Established

Inspired by Prof. Waris Mir's vision, educators and community leaders founded London School in Lahore. The first cohort of students arrived, ready to learn in a warm, nurturing environment.

2012

Robotics Lab Opens

We launched our pioneering Robotics Lab — one of the first in Lahore. Students began building with LEGO, Arduino, and later 3D printers. The future was being made in real time.

2015

AI Study Buddy (Prof Mir Bot) Launches

We introduced our signature AI tool — named after the school's namesake — to personalise learning for every child. Weekly reports to parents began, bringing transparency and partnership.

2018

Cambridge Pathway Certified

London School achieved Cambridge Checkpoint certification, formalising our commitment to world-class curriculum standards. Students could now pursue IGCSE and beyond.

2022

100+ Students & Expanding Faculty

We reached a milestone: 100+ happy families trusting us with their children. Our faculty grew to include specialists in AI, robotics, languages, and childhood development.

2024

Swimming Pool & Multi-Sport Expansion

We expanded our sports programme to 25+ activities and built a covered swimming pool. Every child now has access to world-class facilities and coaching.

2026

The Prof. Waris Mir Campus Today

Today, London School stands as a living monument to Prof. Waris Mir's vision. We continue to innovate, grow, and stay true to the principle that every child matters.
