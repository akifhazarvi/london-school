Our Campus

A Place Kids Love Coming To

Every space at London School is designed to spark curiosity, encourage movement, and make learning feel like an adventure.

Spaces & Facilities

Every Room Has a Purpose

Main Building

Smart Classrooms

Bright, airy classrooms equipped with digital whiteboards, interactive learning tools, and a layout designed for collaboration — not just rows of desks.

Library

The Knowledge Hub

A fully stocked library with reading corners, group study tables, and a stunning mural that captures our philosophy perfectly.

Robotics Lab

Build. Break. Learn.

LEGO Mindstorms, Arduino boards, 3D printers, and a tool wall. Students from Grade 1 upwards get hands-on time here every week.

Computer Lab

AI & Coding Workstations

Dedicated computers for every student, running Scratch, Python, and AI learning environments. Where the next generation of builders starts.

Early Years

Indoor Play Area

A vibrant indoor play space for nursery and KG children — slides, ball pits, and colourful murals make every day feel like a celebration.

Assembly Hall

Events & Gatherings

A spacious hall where the whole school comes together — morning assemblies, student speeches, cultural days, and prize-giving ceremonies.

Music Room

Rhythm & Expression

A dedicated music room where children discover their creative voice — singing, movement, instruments, and performance from an early age.

Sports & Play

25+ Sports Activities

From cricket and football to martial arts and athletics — our outdoor spaces give children the room to run, compete, and build confidence.

Our Flagship Program

Pakistan's Most Advanced Early Robotics Program

From LEGO to Arduino to AI — every child becomes a builder, not just a learner.

Grades 1-3: LEGO Foundations

Young builders start with LEGO Mindstorms and WeDo kits. Problem-solving through play. Engineering concepts without the jargon.

Grades 4-6: Arduino & Sensors

Students code Arduino microcontrollers. Build robots that sense light, temperature, and movement. Real electronics, real impact.

Grades 7-8: 3D Design & AI

CAD design, 3D printing, and introduction to machine learning. Students design, iterate, and create. Competition-ready.

Every Child Gets Hands-On Time

  • Weekly robotics sessions for all grades
  • Competition teams for all interest levels
  • LEGO, Arduino, 3D printers — always available
  • Expert robotics faculty trained in STEM pedagogy
  • Annual robotics showcases where kids demo their creations
  • STEM scholarship opportunities for excellence
