## 1. The 3 Hooks Ranked by Parent Psychology

When Pakistani parents see a Facebook ad for a school, their mind filters it in this order:

1. **Fear of being left behind** (strongest trigger) — "other kids are getting ahead of mine"
2. **Pride** — "imagine my child doing X at age Y"
3. **Value for money** — "best ROI for the fee"

The AI/robotics positioning taps ALL THREE when framed correctly. That's why this is your strongest differentiator — not Cambridge (every school claims it), not foreign languages (nice-to-have), not facilities (table-stakes).

## 4. Landing Page → Ad Message Match

For each hook, create a URL with a matching UTM:
```
enroll.html?utm_source=facebook&utm_campaign=hook1_fomo&utm_content=creative_a
```

This lets you measure in the Google Sheet **which hook produces the best LEADS** (not just cheapest clicks). Over 30 days, you'll know:
- Hook N has Rs X CPL (cheapest)
- Hook M has Rs Y cost-per-campus-visit (best quality)

Scale the winners. Kill the losers.

## 7. Retargeting Audiences to Build in Meta

Create these custom audiences today. They compound value monthly.

| Audience | Who | Why |
|----------|-----|-----|
| **All site visitors last 30 days** | Anyone who loaded any page | Brand-aware warm audience |
| **Visited enroll.html, didn't submit** | 30 days | Hot — one nudge away. Retarget with Hook 4 (urgency) |
| **Time on site > 60s** | 60 days | Quality visitors. Retarget with video ads |
| **Clicked WhatsApp, didn't continue** | 30 days | Medium intent. Retarget with Hook 8 (testimonial) |
| **Scrolled > 75% of homepage** | 60 days | High intent. Retarget with Hook 2 (pride) |
| **Submitted form (Lead event)** | 90 days | Lookalike seed audience. Build 1% LAL for cold targeting |

In 60 days you'll have a warm audience of ~5,000 people you can retarget at Rs 40-60 CPL — 3-4× cheaper than cold.

## 8. What NOT to Say (kills trust)

- ❌ "Best school in Lahore" → unverifiable, every school says this
- ❌ "100% success rate" → impossible, sounds fake
- ❌ "World-class education" → meaningless buzzword
- ❌ "Top teachers" → prove it or don't say it
- ❌ Generic stock photos of happy kids → parents can smell inauthenticity; use real campus photos always
- ❌ Claims of things not in the brochure → the brochure is the source of truth
- ❌ "98% parent satisfaction" → you removed this already. Good. Don't bring it back until there's a real multi-year survey.

## 9. Measurement — What Actually Matters

Stop measuring **cost per lead**. Start measuring **cost per campus visit booked**.

Reasoning: a Rs 180 lead who ghosts is worse than a Rs 400 lead who visits and enrols. The metric chain:

```
Ad click → Page view → Form submit → Campus visit booked → Campus visit attended → Enrolment
      ↓         ↓           ↓                  ↓                      ↓              ↓
    CPM       CPC         CPL            CP-Visit-Booked      CP-Visit-Done     CP-Student
```

Track each step in your Google Sheet + a weekly summary. **Scale what produces cheap enrolments, not cheap leads.**
