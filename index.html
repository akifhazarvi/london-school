Our Legacy

Founded on Prof. Waris Mir's Vision

Prof. Waris Mir (1938-1987) was a visionary educator who believed that true learning happens when curiosity is nurtured, not suppressed. He championed the right to education for every child and advocated for teaching methods that sparked joy and wonder.

Though Prof. Mir passed away decades ago, his spirit lives on in everything we do. The London School System was founded to honor his legacy and continue his mission: creating a generation of confident, kind, and curious learners.
